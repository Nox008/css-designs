Animated Border Cards

A collection of unique, CSS-only animated border effects for cards.

Rotating Gradient

A continuous, multi-colored gradient that spins around the card's edge.

Spotlight Scan

A focused beam of light scans the perimeter, creating a futuristic effect.

Pulsing Glow

The border gently breathes with a soft, glowing pulse animation.

Marching Ants

A classic dashed line effect that animates along the border path.

Hover Draw

The border elegantly draws itself around the card when you hover over it.
